# 🌐 Welcome to DecentraLeziaHub

**Learn Web3, AI & Digital Skills — Completely Free**

This is your free, community-driven learning hub for transitioning into Web3, understanding cryptocurrency and blockchain, mastering AI tools, and building digital skills for the modern economy.

## 🎯 What Is This?

DecentraLeziaHub is a **knowledge hub** designed to:
- ✅ Teach Web3, crypto, and blockchain from scratch
- ✅ Explain AI tools and how to use them
- ✅ Guide your career transition from Web2 to Web3
- ✅ Connect you with opportunities (jobs, airdrops, DAOs)
- ✅ Keep everything **free and accessible**

No prerequisites. No paywalls. Just knowledge.
